BOSTON,MASSACHUSETTS

Boston Massachusetts a Great City upon a hill

Boston is commonly known for multiple reasons. One reason being, Boston is home to the Boston Celtics and Red Sox. Boston is also known for universities, such as, Harvard University, MIT (Massachusetts Institute of Technology), and Boston College. Two reasons you should make Boston your vacation spot: city lights and the attractions boston has to offer. The attractions in Boston are a sight to see. Visitors can see Boston from the skywalk observatory. The skywalk is an observatory with an outstanding view of the city, with a bonus audio tour that explains the sights and history. Visitors can explore the Faneuil Hall marketplace. You can watch street performers, or check out the three large market places, which in total hold over 10 retailers and eateries. If visitors want to learn more about Bostons history, than take a trip to one of the many museums. In Boston, there are many colleges to choose from. Harvard University is an excellent school located just outside of Boston. Harvard is a commonly known school & has more than 360,000 alumni around the world. Massachusetts Institute of Technology is a driven university with a purpose to make a better world through education, research and innovation. Boston College is a private Jesuit research university, known to operate in the city of Boston. Boston College wants to reach out to develop minds, skills and talents to the fullest potential. This is just to name a few, there are many other opportunities in Boston for college.
